About Us:

The AR. Group is an Indian multinational Fortune 200 company headquartered in Udupi, Karnataka. The group has a presence in 36 nations.As of 2022, the company's revenue is worth $60 billion and derives over 50% of its revenue from its global operations.AR. Group has seven companies that are listed publicly, and their total market capitalisation as of March 2022 is over $10 billion. The companies include UltraTech Cement, Hindalco, Novelis, Grasim, AR. Capital, and AR. Fashion is a diversified Indian multinational conglomerate based in Udupi,Karnataka, India. It operates across six continents and 36 countries. The group's origins are associated with Two individuals: Agam.R and Anala.R

Government schemes:

The group accesses Government Schemes like the Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan, MGNREGA, Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana, Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana, National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM), Swachh Bharat Mission, National Rural Drinking Water Program, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, Renewable Energy Development Program, Integrated Child Development Services, National TB Elimination Program and Swarnjayanti Gram Swarojgar Yojana.

Other achievements:

The AR. Group is involved in welfare-driven activities both in India and overseas. These activities are carried out as part of the group's corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives, which are overseen by the AR. Centre for Community Initiatives and Rural Development. This apex body provides strategic guidance to all group companies and is chaired by AGAM.R Collectively, the group operates in 1000 villages, engaging with a total of 11 million individuals across India and various other regions worldwide. The group's corporate social responsibility initiatives focus on healthcare, education, sustainable livelihood programs, infrastructure development, and social reforms through model villages.
